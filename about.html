About Me

who is n0signal?

I’m a live events technician turned cybersecurity learner, documenting my journey from backstage to digital defense.

Background

I started out in technical theatre and live events, designing and running lighting, sound, and networked control systems. Working in that world meant dealing with high-pressure moments where things had to work — and it taught me how to stay composed, solve problems fast, and keep systems reliable when the stakes were high.

Why Cyber Security?

The same mindset that kept shows alive now drives my cybersecurity work: anticipating problems, breaking things apart to understand them, and building reliable solutions. I’ve been expanding my skills through TryHackMe, HackTheBox, homelab experiments, and formal study.

What This Site Is

This portfolio is where I share projects, write-ups on challenges and labs, and longer reflections in the blog. I also maintain a structured knowledge base of my learning process. It’s a work in progress, but it’s my way of stacking wins and tracking growth.
